Getting Around

The venue is quite far out of the city so is not accesible by public transport. Please use one of the below to get to the venue.

Car

If you have a car or have hired a car then the accomodation and venue both have parking.

miniBUD

This is a popular airport transfer service that you can book in advance.

For more info check out their website.

Public Transport

Budapest has great public transport links to almost all parts of the city except the airport (it's just one bus) and the venue!

That being said if you want to do some site seeing I would reccomend City Mapper App & the Budapest Go App

Budapest Go allows you to buy tickets on your phone with card, to use the tickets there are QR codes on the OUTSIDE of Buses/Trams/Trolley Buses and at the entrace of Metro Stations.

Uber & Bolt

App based travel has finally arrived to the Hungarian capital, both Uber and Bolt run. I only have experience with bolt but it is well priced and pretty fast to get a ride.
